Santa Teresita Bakery - Site Plan

Site Name

Site Name: Santa Teresita Bakery

This name represents a traditional family bakery founded in 1953 in Yucatán. It reflects trust, craftsmanship, and community recognition.

Optional Domain: santateresita-bakery.com

Site Purpose

The purpose of this website is to present the bakery’s history, showcase its products, and provide contact information for customers.

It will also strengthen brand identity and promote traditional artisanal baking.

Scenarios

  • What types of bread and pastries does Santa Teresita Bakery offer?
  • Where is the bakery located and what are its business hours?
  • How can I place a special order for an event?

Color Schema

The selected colors represent warmth, tradition, and reliability. These colors are applied throughout this document.

  • Dark Brown (#5C3A21): Headings and main text
  • Light Beige (#F5E6D3): Page background
  • Soft Gold (#C9A14A): Borders and accents
  • Yellow (#FFDE21): Header and footer

Typography

  • Arial: Main body text and paragraphs
  • Playfair Display: Headings and titles
  • Sans-serif: Fallback font
